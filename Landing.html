What is MindChat ?

MindChat is an open source website , made by students to help undergraduates USTHB students in their studies.

Got many questions and need answers?

YOU ARE IN THE RIGHT PLACE!

In MindChat students can ask questions in different fields of their studies, and get answers from other students, 'BUT' in enthusiastic way...

The main goal is to share knowledge between student, and help each other, in a competitive atmosphere.
"Knowledge is that which benefits
not that which is memorized." Imam al-Shafi'i

points rank system

  • For each question you answer , you will gain 1 point.
  • When you gain 100 points, you will unlock a Quizz, this Quizz will give you additional points.
  • there will be a Rank system : "Bronze","Silver","Gold".you can reach them after specific number of points.
